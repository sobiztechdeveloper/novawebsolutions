1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work properly and help us understand how people use our website.

Nova Web Solutions (PVT) LTD may use cookies to:

  • Improve website functionality and performance.

3. Analytics Cookies

If analytics is used, cookies may collect non-sensitive data such as page views, time on page, device type, and general location information. This helps us improve content and user experience.

4. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to manage or disable cookies in settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.
